Facility Management

Integrated management for efficient, safe, and organized facilities

Smart Touch provides integrated facility management services designed to ensure the optimal operation of buildings and sites, improve performance efficiency, protect assets, and support business continuity through a professional operating model focused on quality, safety, and cost control.

What this service covers

  • Daily building operations and full site coordination.
  • Supervision of cleaning, maintenance, and support services.
  • Vendor and contractor coordination and follow-up.
  • Asset and equipment monitoring.
  • Handling reports, complaints, and emergency cases.
  • Monitoring energy and water consumption to improve efficiency and reduce waste.

Our methodology

  • Comprehensive site assessment before starting service.
  • Tailored operational plan according to facility nature and client priorities.
  • Preventive and periodic maintenance schedules.
  • Continuous supervision and documented operational follow-up.
  • Periodic management reports covering performance and quality indicators.

Value to the client

  • Improved operational efficiency and reduced service interruptions.
  • Longer asset life cycle and lower repair frequency.
  • Reduced operating costs through better planning and control.
  • One reliable service partner coordinating multiple functions.
  • A safer, cleaner, and better organized environment.
